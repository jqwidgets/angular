Smart Paste in jqxForm

Smart Paste lets users quickly fill out forms by pasting structured data—like from spreadsheets or tables—directly into the form. It intelligently maps the pasted content to matching fields, saving time and reducing errors.

To enable this feature, set the following properties:
enableSmartPaste: true
aiKey: 'your-openai-api-key'
aiUrl: 'https://your-server-endpoint'
